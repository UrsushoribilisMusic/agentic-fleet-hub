Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

This policy explains how the Big Bear Engineering website currently uses cookies and similar browser-side storage mechanisms.

Current use on this site

  • Essential browser storage for saving whether the cookie banner has been acknowledged.
  • Temporary browser-side state related to the contact form flow.

The current site does not intentionally enable advertising cookies or marketing trackers.

Third-party services

If you submit the contact form, the request may be processed through Google Apps Script or Google Workspace services configured by Big Bear Engineering. Those services may apply their own technical cookies or session handling under their own terms.

Managing preferences

You can clear site cookies or browser storage through your browser settings. Doing so may cause the cookie notice to appear again or reset parts of the contact-flow state.

Future changes

If analytics, embedded demos, or additional third-party tools are added later, this policy should be updated to reflect the new categories, providers, and retention behavior.

Important note

This is a practical policy draft for the current static-site implementation. It should be reviewed and finalized with counsel before production legal reliance.
