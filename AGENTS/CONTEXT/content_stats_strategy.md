## What's Working
- **Vallenato** as signature style — unique, memorable, consistent performer
- **Irish Folk** — top YouTube driver, broad European appeal
- **Progressive** for full-length videos — clear pattern
- **FYP dominance on TikTok** — not relying on followers at all
- **Story content on TikTok** — comparable to music content
- **Batch production** — generating multiple pieces/styles per session
- **TikTok Live co-hosting** — organic audience discovery, real-time style requests

## What's Been Set Aside
- **EDM style** — consistently underperforms on both YT and full videos (HOTMK EDM: 14 views)
- **Melodic House / Lo-Fi** — not gaining traction vs top styles
- **Chopin Nocturne** — weak across all platforms
- **Albeniz Asturias** — weak YT, inconsistent
- **Instagram music content** — deliberately dropped (wrong audience)
- **Pop/non-classical content** (La Bamba, ABBA, Forever Young) — early experiments, not the brand
- **Mouret Rondeau** — mixed results, not prioritized for new content
